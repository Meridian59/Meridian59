Alzahakar

Councilman in the Hermit's Hut

Actual Description

BACK STORY

One of the oldest friends of King Zarcos V, Alzahakar has been counseling the princess since birth. An amalgamation of the middle eastern sorcerer Jafar and Obi-Wan Kenobi, he has mastered all magic except Jala and Riija. Having the will of four gods in him, he is very careful about how he wields his knowledge. Excruciating balance is the only way he can avoid the wrath of any one deity. If Alzahakar has a flaw, it is his ego finding himself above all lesser magicians.
