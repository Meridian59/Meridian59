Actual Description

BACK STORY

Colhorr is a man of very few words, mostly because he's deaf from too many years of the constant clanging of sledge hammer on iron. He doesn't pick up much around him, and consequently doesn't have much to offer. He is in his late fifties and putters about with the slow familiarity of a man very comfortable in his own environment. He is large and bulky but quite short, gray bearded, shirtless in heavy trousers. He generates most of his business by fabricating and repairing plows, hoes, shovels and other farming implements, and in truth makes pretty sub-standard weapons and armor.
