Paddock

Bartender of Familiars in Tos

As owner of perhaps the most popular bar in the world, Paddock goes out of his way to smile at every customer who comes through the door. His down to earth demeanor and common man approach to his clientele makes him one of the most likable men in Tos.

BACK STORY

Paddock realizes he runs the most popular tavern in the world. He is both cocky and friendly. To keep the bar alive, he has become known as quite a rumor monger, spreading tales about everything and everyone though he tries to keep a positive spin on everything. The only thing Paddock will speak poorly of is Barloque. He does not consider the other inns to be a threat to his business. Paddock might be played by Harvey Keitel.
