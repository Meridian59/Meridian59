Spells

Shal'ille

  • Detect Evil
    • level 2
    • Type: Self enchantment
    • Effect: player sees an aura or other effect around a monster with a karma below 0 or a player with a karma below -20.
  • Martyr's Battleground
    • level 3
    • type: room enchantment
    • Effect: any player kill that occurs in this room has an additional 50%-150% chance of creating a revenant.
  • Identify
    • level 3
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: Caster can see all true attributes that an item has, included any hidden ones.
  • QorBane
    • level 4
    • type: room enchantment
    • Effect: any Qor spellcaster in the room takes 1-10 points of damage any time they cast a Qor spell.
  • Spirit Hammer
    • Level 4
    • Type: sorcery
    • Effect: creates a blessed hammer which stays in existence for 30-90 seconds.
  • Reveal
    • level 5
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: permanently strips away all wards hiding item attributes.
  • Raise Dead
    • Level 6
    • Type: sorcery
    • Effect: teleports a player from the underworld to their corpse. Caster casts spell by targetting the corpse.

Kraanan

  • Resist Poison
    • level 2
    • type: self enchantment
    • effect: player with resist poison cast is 5%-75% less likely to get poisoned. This spell does nothing about existing poisoned effects.
  • Detect Invisible
    • level 3
    • type: enchantment
    • effect: a player sees some sort of effect which allows them to see invisible people, monsters and items.
  • Haste
    • level 3
    • type: self enchantment
    • effect: a player can run with 1-100% less of a vigor loss.
  • Silence
    • level 4
    • type: room enchantment
    • effect: no one may talk or yell in this room, although they may use tells and sends to communicate.
  • Shroud
    • level 4
    • type: weapon enchantment
    • effect: weapon may not be further enchanted for X amount of time.
  • Dispel Illusion
    • level 6
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: has a 1-100% chance of shattering each illusion in the room.

Faren

  • Wall of Thorns
    • level 2
    • type: create monster
    • effect: creates an object where the spellcaster stands, which does 1-5 points of damage to whoever walks into it, and also prevents them from passing. Wall of thorns can be attacked, but will not attack back, and has 30-100 hit points. It will fade on it's own in 30-200 seconds.
  • Fog
    • level 3
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: fills room with visual fog. Also reduces range of missile weapons by 5-50%.
  • Wall of Flame
    • level 4
    • type: create monster
    • effect: creates an object where the spellcaster stands, which does 1-20 points of damage to anyone who walks upon it (spellcaster is immune). Can be walked through, however, it cannot be killed. Wall of flame lasts for 30-200 seconds
  • Spore Cloud
    • level 5
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: paralyzes everyone in the room, including the caster, for 30-60 seconds. Caster may be paralyzed longer than the others.

Qor

  • Detect Good
    • level 2
    • type: enchantment
    • effect: Draws an aura around any monster with a karma of greater than 0, and any player with a karma greater than 20.
  • Animate
    • level 3
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: Replaces a corpse with a pet zombie of level 1-50, which will defend the caster but move very very slowly.
  • Curse Weapon
    • level 3
    • type: enchant weapon
    • effect: places a curse on a weapon, making it impossible to drop without help.
  • ShalilleBane
    • level 4
    • type: room enchantment
    • effect: Anyone who casts a Shal'ille spell loses 1-20 points of vigor.
  • Deathlink
    • level 5
    • type: room enchantment
    • effect: Caster gains a bonus of + 1-8HP and mana any time someone in the room dies - doubled if that something is a player.
  • Paralyze
    • level 6
    • type: trance enchantment
    • effect: target player remains held until either he takes damage or the caster does.

Jala

  • Warp Time
    • level 2
    • type: song (trance room enchantment)
    • effect:  all trance spells take 50-250% longer to go off.
  • Truth
    • level 3
    • type: song (trance room enchantment)
    • effect: shatters all illusions upon casting. As long as the caster remains in a trance, Riija spells have a 1-100% additional chance of failure.
  • SpellBane
    • level 4
    • type: song (trance room enchantment)
    • effect: no spells may be cast in this room as long as caster holds trance.

Riija

  • Anonymous
    • level 1
    • type: self enchantment
    • effect: player's name disappears from over his head for 30-300 seconds.
  • Flash
    • level 1
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: bright flashes all in room. Has a 1-100% chance of cancelling any trances in the room.
  • Distract
    • level 2
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: attempts to get monsters in the room to chase caster. Success rate is 1-100% for monsters without targets, and 1-50% for monsters who are already targetting someone.
  • Foresight
    • level 2
    • type: sorcery
    • effect: Returns a list of 1-5 spells and or skills target has, and how good he is at it.
  • Illusionary Wounds
    • level 2
    • type: enchantment
    • effect: When initially cast, target loses 1-15 life. This is returned when the enchantment fades (15-30 seconds later).
  • Apparition 
    • level 3
    • type: create monster
    • effect: creates a common monster (but with only one hit point and no treasure) and has it chase the target.
  • Counterfeit
    • level 3
    • type: create token
    • effect: creates a fake token for the princess/duke scenario. Token will last for 5-30 minutes. Giving the token to the princess, duke or councilor has a 15% chance of being treated like an actual token, but is more likely to be ignored, or even of angering the councilor.
  • Wall of Illusion
    • level 3
    • type: create wall
    • effect: creates a fire wall that does no damage when it is walked into. Wall lasts 30-200 seconds.
  • Vertigo
    • level 3
    • type: enchant player
    • effect: blurs the target's viewpoint. Also reduces that player's fighting stats (agility, strength and aim) by 1-20 points for 10-60 seconds.
  • Forget
    • level 4
    • type: enchant player
    • effect: target player forgets 1-5 spells at random, for 45-300 seconds.
  • Denial
    • level 4
    • type: enchant self
    • effect: self is healed 1-100 health points. After 15-45 seconds, self loses that much health.
  • Mirror Image
    • level 4
    • type: create monster
    • effect: creates a monster which looks like the caster, and which will defend the caster.
  • Artifice
    • level 4
    • type: enchant item
    • effect: makes the item appear to have an attribute that it does not have. Can also mask existing attributes.
  • Personal Apparition
    • level 5
    • type: create monster
    • effect: creates a monster, which looks like a high level common monster with 1-20 hit points, which only the target can see.
  • Morph
    • level 5
    • type: enchant self
    • effect: change own appearance to look like a certain monster. Automatically gets rid of that person's name, and possibly gives some attributes to that player's attack (such as spider poisoning).
