Magic Potions:

general notes:

  • All of the following potions simply implement existing spell effects, with a random spellpower from 25-75. This range may need to be adjusted for some potions.
  • Potions have, for the most part, not yet been assigned an appropriate value.
  • The reveal spell should, in principle, identify these potions but I haven't been able to get it to work yet.
  • note that many of the potions are the same color and have the same description, so as to necessitate either getting them identified or gambling on it.
  • None of the new potions have been placed for sale or as monster/quest treasure.

Magic Weapons:

  • There is one new magic weapon itemattribute, which has a certain percentage chance of enacting a particular spell effect on a target hit by the weapon.
  • Such weapon attributes are generated randomly when monster treasure is created, just like with other weapon attributes.
  • When the attribute is first added to a weapon, a spell effect is randomly chosen from a list of possible spell effects, and a power is chosen randomly for the weapon.
  • the power of the weapon ranges from 10 to 50.
    • the percentage chance that a hit will enact the spell effect is (power/10).
    • The spellpower of the resulting spell effect is (power).
  • The weapon can enact the following spell effects upon its target:
    • blind
    • dement
    • enfeeble
    • vertigo
    • purify
    • swap
    • invisibility
    • forget
    • evil twin
    • mark of dishonor
