New Spells for Renaissance:

[Draft 4: Oct 9, 97]

This list is split into three levels of priority. A's get done before B's, C's are pretty much a wish-list and are likely to fall off the schedule.

note: there is a new type of spell, summoning. This means any type of spell which creates objects in the room. This includes wall of fire, reflection, fog, web, etc. Any spell of this type must count other summoned objects in the room before allowing the spell to be cast. The max number should be roughly equivalent to two walls/minefields, three webs/fogs, x reflections. This has already been implemented in a nonstandard way with reflection and firewalls, but since this update contains a number of new spells of this type, it needs to be standardized.

[Tim: this means it might be warranted to create a new kind of passive object which firewall, web, mine, and other objects will subclass. Dealing with reflections and attackable bramble objects will require additional handling, but most things would fit under that umbrella.]

[Tim: mana, vigor, etc numbers here are first-pass estimates. If you think some need tweaking, I defer to your judgement. Let me know if you want to adjust them majorly though.]

A-list:

Faren:

Web: The floor is covered with a sticky substance, and any player or monster (except spiders) who walks through it may get stuck (3x3 square of hotplates). While stuck, player can turn, attack, cast spells, but cannot move. Web objects are visible on ground, and a stuck player has a graphical overlay of webbing on legs and feet. Chances of getting stuck and duration are proportional to spellpower.

    kod details:

    • level: 3
    • mana: 15
    • vigor: 10
    • trance: 3 seconds
    • reagents: 1 web moss (of course)
    • type: summoning
    • increase chance: 25
    • outlaw: yes
    • teacher: tendrath (new ghost npc)
    • duration: stuck players held for about 2/3 the duration of the hold spell. web objects stick around (ha!) for about 2/3 as long as firewall objects.

    art needs:

    • spell list icon
    • enchantment icon for stuck players (can be same as spell list icon).
    • one-view object of webs on ground. Wide enough to occupy a little more than a room square (same width as firewall objects), not very tall (need to look like they're flat against the ground).
    • one-view player overlay for stuck players (same for all six player views). Webbing extending from the ground up the player's legs to about knee-level.
