Konima's Island Room Details

KC1

The Chasm

A great, deep chasm splits up the jungle here. There is a bridge to cross leading to Quest NPC IV. She is a crazed Avar and has put the skulls and other trophies of her kills at the entrance to the bridge to scare off intruders. Crossing this bridge is the only access to KD1, home of Quest NPC IV.
