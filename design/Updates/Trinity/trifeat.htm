Trinity Feature List

Volume I:

  • Adjust the parameters of the faction scenario to prepare for the third faction and to improve the position of the unfactioned.
  • Jonas begins to hang out in Jasper more.
  • Bands of brigands, rebels, and faction troops in the countryside.
  • Ability to summon bands of humans for events.
  • A small number of cannon-fodder NPCs wandering around towns. (B)
  • New art for Barloque and Jasper.
  • New monster: groundworm (three variants: one half-size, two full-size color-variants).

Volume II:

  • Third faction.
  • Shrines, with new areas and associated puzzles.
  • Miriana visits nodes.
  • Miriana attacks nodes (with Xeochicatls).
  • New art for Marion and some of the countryside.
  • New monster: Xeochicatl (four color-variants on one basic body).

Volume III:

  • New art for Cor Noth.
  • New wander coordinates for living and ghostly refugees from Cor Noth.
  • Book in Cor Noth with 'teach' spell.
  • Mechanics for player councillors.
  • Mechanics for councillor powers:
    • Ability to adjust shop prices of different categories of item.
    • Ability to trade mana regen rate for global spellpower boost.
    • Ability to trade hp regen rate for vigor regen rate.
    • Ability to trade atrophy in particular schools for learning boost (shared by spell and skill councillors).
  • Miriana attacks shrines (with Dark angels).
  • New area: Councillors' meeting chamber.
  • New monster: Dark angel.
