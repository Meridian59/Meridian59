Fehr'loi Qan

Blacksmith Barloque

Though well renown throughout the land for his superb skill as a master weapon smith, Fehr’loi Qan himself could not be considered the sharpest sword on the rack. The large man’s slow speech seems out of place in the high brow city of Barloque. You get the impression that he is uncomfortable with strangers, but his great size and clear devotion to Kraanan makes him afraid of no one.

BACK STORY

Plain and simple, a dim wit. Fehr’loi Qan is one of the finest Blacksmiths in the mainland, but is nearly as well known for his slow speech. He is uncomfortable with strangers, but his large size and clear devotion to Kraanan, makes him afraid of no one. He has moved his shop from Tos to Barloque. The reasons are many: uncomfort with being so close to the graveyard of Tos, higher prices being offered in Barloque, a falling out with the Duke and a bribe by the Princess. Fehr'loi Qan has an intense interest in magical wands and has found unusual ways to utilize them to craft his weapons and armor, such as using a fire wand to heat up metal more than his forge can. Long ago, Fehr'loi Qan took a pilgrimage to the Temple of Kraanan to learn how the great weapons of Kraanan were made centuries ago. During his visit, the priestess taught him a simple spell of Magic Shield, which he will now teach to anyone who asks.

MOOD FACTOR

Strangers and talk of Tos make Fehr'loi Qan uncomfortable and decrease his mood factor,
People who frequent his shop and magic wands tend to put him in a better mood.
