Bahal zax'Ot

Mad Scientist on Island

Leering at you as if you were a potential subject for some twisted experiment, Bahal smiles and chortles to himself. The moody little man seems unnaturally obsessed with the horrific and the bizarre

BACK STORY

This moody elder man is obsessed with his secret experiments. He does not take well to visitors and tends to make them paranoid by treating them like potential objects of experimentation. His room should be full of bizarre items, dead animal, and mysterious apparatuses. His hawk nose and beady eyes seem to take apart the players as he makes overly intuitive surmises of them (what items they have, whether they are good or evil, when they last ate, whatever can be programmed). · Temple Quest NPC · Might give player random (bad) potions or other experiments.
