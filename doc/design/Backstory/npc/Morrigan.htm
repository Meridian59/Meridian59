Morrigan

Innkeeper at The Limping Toad in Marion

Actual Description

BACK STORY

The second part of the comedy duo with Tova, Morrigan is a giggling simpleton. Lovable, grinning fool. He runs a shop buying and selling sundries and reagents in the same building as the tavern, but you get the feeling that his heart really isn't in it, he keeps his little shop open just to be near his hero, Tova. He just can't wait to see what's going to come out of the mans mouth next. Morrigan is short and slight, wearing similar country grab to Tova but without an apron, pants made perhaps in a tartan wool.
