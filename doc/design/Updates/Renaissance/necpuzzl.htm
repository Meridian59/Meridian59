Necromancer Entrance Puzzle

Part 1

Located in G9, 'an ancient place, its origin forgotten'.

Backstory:

This is the ancient arena of Brax, the dark twin of the Tos arena. While the Tos arena is dedicated to Kraanan and exists to glorify honorable combat, the Brax arena was dedicated to Qor, and existed to glorify the senseless slaughter of helpless victims. After hundreds of years of disuse since the city's fall, some of the arena's enchantments have weakened and drained away, but for the most part it still works.

Players will find a first-hand account of the horrors of the arena of Brax, either written in a tome somewhere on the mainland or in the Bards' Tales of Adventure (TBD). Brendon will craft this creepy bit of prose, but it will be along these lines:

    I was disguised as a necromancer, hidden in the crowd standing on the center platform of the arena of Brax. The Evil Queen raised her hands to the roiling clouds above, and a bolt of lightning slammed down into the central pillar. The earth shook, there was the sound of stone grating against stone, I struggled through the crowd to see what was going on below. I was thrown back as more lighting struck, once, twice, thrice, maybe more, then I managed to break through to the edge of the platform just as I felt it begin to rise. Far below the stands, the unarmed, helpless victims huddled together, terrified. A horde of deformed skeletal warriors swarmed into view, and with an inhuman cry descended upon the humans, tearing them limb from limb as the necromancers cackled with glee. I shuddered and withdrew from the gruesome spectacle below, vowing that I would see this abomination destroyed.

This is all the information the players will receive.
