New Monsters

Lich Queen:

  • boss creature, strength comparable to Lupogg King.
  • the only monster in the game who also acts as an npc, ie talking and selling stuff.
  • When killed, all necromancers are killed and the Order of Necromancers is disbanded.
  • very evil.

Battered Skeleton:

  • slightly less powerful than a normal skeleton.
  • can cause poison or dementia.
  • evil

Tusked Skeleton:

  • slightly more powerful than a normal skeleton.
  • evil.

Horned Skeleton:

  • way more powerful than a normal skeleton.
  • moves fast.
  • very evil.

Narthyl Worm:

  • moves slow.
  • drains vigor with each hit.
  • neutral karma.
  • creepy slithery animation.

Black Spider:

  • highly poisonous (more so than normal spider)
  • a lot tougher than a normal spider.
  • slightly evil karma (same as spider).
