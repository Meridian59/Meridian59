More New Objects for Renaissance

[Draft 1: Oct 28, 97]

A List

3 Crystal Prisms (item)

Focal items for multi-caster spells. One prism with 3 faces (triangular prism), one with 5 faces, one with 7 faces. The number of faces limits the number of people who can help out with the spell.

art needs:

  • inventory/look view.
  • dropped one-view.

Torch (item)

To be held in a player's shield hand.

art needs:

  • inventory/look view (already exists)
  • dropped one-view (already exists)
  • third-person player overlay, six views plus attack animations.
  • no first-person window overlay needed.

Skulls (ornamental object)

A few ornamental objects, the skulls from the new skeletons, to scatter around the necropolis for atmosphere.

art needs:

  • third-person one-view.

Demon (event npc)

A big, red devil npc for events. Big chest, little waist, hoofs for feet, reddish skin, big evil sharp-toothed grin.

art needs:

  • third-person one-view.

Dragon Skull (event item)

Event-only rare prize item. Large skull of a dragon.

art needs:

  • inventory/look one-view
  • dropped view

Panther-man (event npc)

Half man, half beast. head of a panther, body of a man, clawed hands. (can also be a wolf-man, koala-man, ostrich-man... oh, wait. um, scratch the last two.)

art needs:

  • third-person one-view.

Urn or Bowl (ornamental object)

An ornamental ancient urn or bowl filled with unidentifiable yucky things. For decorating the sacrificial table in the necromancer guild hall.

  • third-person one-view.

Mystical Tome (ornamental object)

Creepy black-magic tome, open and closed views, ornamental object for the sacrificial table in the necromancer guild hall.

  • third-person one-view, closed.
  • third-person one-view, open.

Goat Head (ornamental object)

a goat head ornamental object to hang on the wall in the necromancer guild hall.

  • third-person one-view.
