New Quests

Quest 44 : QST_ID_ESTABLISH_NECROGUILD

  • Offered by the Lich Queen Venya'cyr, only after a certain number (currently 5) of necromancer amulets are in the world.
  • She only offers it to necromancers (someone using an 'amulet of the three').
  • She doesn't offer it if you've tried recently.
  • steps:
    • you show up.
    • she asks if you want the quest.
    • you say yes.
    • she asks for blood of the innocent.
    • you give it to her, and she is reborn and you are now the Master of the Order of Necromancers.

Quest 45: QST_ID_APHRODESIAC

  • not offered if you've tried recently.
  • steps:
    • you say 'need a courier?'
    • [madelia, or kraanan priestess] asks you to ask [Frisconar, Miriana, Joguer, or Lady Aftyn] about an aphrodesiac.
    • that npc asks you for a queen spider egg.
    • you find one and bring it back.
    • the second npc gives you a potion for the first npc.
    • you deliver it, and receive your reward.

Quest 46: QST_ID_NECRO_WARNING

  • not offered if you've tried to recently.
  • not offered to newbies.
  • only offered to evil players.
  • steps:
    • you say 'need a courier?'
    • Maleval gives you a letter to give to the [Shal'ille, Kranaan, Qor, or Faren ] priestess.
    • you do so, they call you crazy.
      • if you fail, you get a curse on you.
    • you return to maleval, he gives you stuff.
