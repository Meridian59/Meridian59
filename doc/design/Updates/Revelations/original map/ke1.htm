Konima's Island Room Details

KE1

The Bridge of Faith

The jungle fades into a rocky cliff where the worn path ends and the Temple of Riija can be seen in the distance on a small island. The only way to get to the temple is to cross the magical invisible Bridge of Faith that begins here. Two large stone totems mark the first step so that seekers of the knowledge of Riija know where to begin their journey across the winding invisible bridge ahead.
