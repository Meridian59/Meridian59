Changes/Features in Dark Auspices

  • Lag deaths were removed from the game.
  • Disarm will stun monsters.
  • Cannot use spell items (potions, wands, and scrolls) while under the effect of the hold spell.
  • All spell items are karma sensitive, including potions.
  • Can now cast area of effect spells with "illegal" targets in the area if the spells will not affect the target. EG, a player can now cast firewall around newbies and in Sacred Haven with other players in the area.
  • DMs and higher cannot be attacked if they are immortal and cannot advance.
  • Riija swords slowly heal if you cast Riija spells while wielding it (not over maximum).
  • Wedding rings won't drop upon owner's death anymore.
  • Outlaw status not removed for a cheap (EG, token) death.
  • Advancement timer will not reset for cheap (EG, token) deaths.
  • No faction loss or karma changes from hitting players during Frenzies.
  • Weapons are now a factor of type and quality.
  • Punch does more damage and takes less vigor.
  • Weapons now have a bit longer range, particularly swords.
  • Aim has more of an effect on hitting than before, roughly double.
  • Spells are now more dependant on the requiste stat for spell power than before. (Previously, it basically assumed you had a 50 for your requisite stat).
  • Pants no longer shatter in the heat of battle, they fall to tatters.
  • Loot from faction soldiers not available immediately.
  • Faction soldiers drop less items.
  • Shatter wands will be rarer and have less charges (thanks to a few bugs being fixed).
  • Can no longer get parry bonuses or improves with a ranged weapon equipped.
  • Primary chance to succeed no longer recieves a bonus from intellect.
  • Secondary chance to advance in a skill will no longer be negative, it is bound so that under relatively normal conditions, minimum secondary chance to advance is 5%.
