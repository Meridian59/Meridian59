Patch Spell Revisions

Objectives of the redesign for spells:

  • Link the effectiveness of all spells to the spellpower with which the spell is cast.
  • Make all personal enchantment spells targetable spells to encourage co-operation.
  • Establish a consistant damage vs. cost/level ratio for attack spell groups (touch spells, wall spell, etc . . .).
  • Make lame spells that nobody ever uses cool.

The progress so far.

What Brian's been upto.

Notation:
  • text in itlaics denotes a B-list change. (Usually due to dependency on other game systems, i.e. An AI change.)
  • green text denotes a new spell school and level.
  • red text denotes a new value for the relevent field. (e.g. 1 Elderberry would indicate that the spell will now require 1 elderberry.)
  • + indicates an small increase in the current value, ++ indicates a great increase and +++ indicates more than double current value.
  • - indicates an small decrease in the current value, -- indicates a great decrease and --- indicates less than half current value.
All changes are open to discussion and spell school/level changes in particular are not final.
